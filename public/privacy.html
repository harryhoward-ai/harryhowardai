DashFun Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-01-01

DashFun (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides an AI-powered gaming platform that blends Web2 and Web3 experiences. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: email address, Telegram ID, Apple ID, or wallet address.
  • Gameplay Data: scores, preferences, avatars, and progress.
  • Device Information: IP address, device model, OS version, app version, language settings, and crash logs.
  • Payment Data: limited info from in-app purchases (handled by Apple).
  • Web3 Data: public blockchain address and NFT holdings (read-only).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize game experiences
  • Sync progress across devices
  • Analyze gameplay to improve performance and balance
  • Verify transactions and deliver digital assets
  • Detect fraud and enforce community rules

3. Sharing and Third-Party Services

We may share anonymized or aggregate data with analytics providers or to comply with legal requirements.

We may integrate with:

  • Apple In-App Purchase system
  • Telegram Login or Apple ID
  • Third-party analytics tools (e.g., Firebase, Amplitude)

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Web3 and Wallet Usage

If you choose to use Web3 features:

  • We only read public blockchain data
  • You control your assets
  • We never request or store private keys or seed phrases

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at support@dashfun.games or through the app's Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

DashFun is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and notify you via the app or our website.
